Unmask ownership

Two views, because the two source datasets give different levels of transparency. Subsidized housing (NHPD) reports real owner and manager names. General private housing (MOD-IV) does not include owner names in this extract — so that section instead surfaces shared mailing addresses, the standard signal for a single owner (often an LLC or a registered agent) controlling many parcels under different names. See the methodology page for details.
